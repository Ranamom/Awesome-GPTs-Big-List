# [Hacker News Guru](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-2HNp4UjwN-hacker-news-guru)

Hacker News Guru is an App that provides you with curated news and articles from the world of hacking and cybersecurity. Stay updated with the latest trends, techniques, and vulnerabilities. Get access to a wide range of resources, including tutorials, blog posts, and research papers. The App allows you to browse through different categories, search for specific topics, and even save articles for later reading. With Hacker News Guru, you can level up your knowledge and stay one step ahead in the exciting world of hacking!
