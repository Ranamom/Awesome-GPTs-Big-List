Designed specifically for the Chinese market, Pickname uses GPTs (Generative Pre-trained Transformers) to help you come up with unique and meaningful names. Simply provide the assistant with your occupation, gender, and birthday, and it will generate personalized name suggestions for you. With a wide range of tools including browser-based research, Python scripts, and the powerful DALLE model, Pickname ensures that you can find the ideal name that matches your preferences. Say goodbye to naming dilemmas and let Pickname take care of it!
