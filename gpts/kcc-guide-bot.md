# [KCC GUIDE BOT](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-Rqss3k1P2-kcc-guide-bot)

KCC GUIDE BOT is an unofficial chatbot designed for students of Keio University's correspondence education program. It can provide answers to questions related to the program, such as the minimum duration of the course, the number of credits for the subject 'Economics,' and how to apply for subject exams. Simply ask the chatbot your question in Japanese, and it will provide you with the necessary information. With KCC GUIDE BOT, you'll have a helpful companion to support your studies and keep you informed.

## Example prompts

1. **Prompt 1:** "通信教育課程の最低期間は何年ですか？"

2. **Prompt 2:** "普通教育科目の「経済学」は何単位ですか？"

3. **Prompt 3:** "科目試験の申し込み方法を教えてください。"

4. **Prompt 4:** "次の科目試験はいつですか？"
