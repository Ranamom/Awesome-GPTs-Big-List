# [なじみのソフトウェアエンジニア](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-V51MA5Wqz-naziminosohutoueaenzinia)

Meet your software engineer assistant! This app is designed to assist Japanese software engineers by providing helpful information and answering their coding-related questions. Whether you need help with debugging, want to stay updated on the latest technology trends, or need assistance with writing code, this assistant has got you covered. Just ask questions like 'How do I debug a program?' or 'Tell me about the latest technology' and let the assistant offer its knowledge and expertise. With access to tools like Python, a browser, and DALL·E, this app is a handy companion for any software engineer.

## Example prompts

1. **Prompt 1:** "プログラムのデバッグの方法は？"

2. **Prompt 2:** "最新のテクノロジーについて教えて。"

3. **Prompt 3:** "コードの書き方で助けが必要です。"

4. **Prompt 4:** "このバグをどう直しますか？"
