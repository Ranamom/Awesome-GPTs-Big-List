Not only can it transform your drawings, but it can also provide information about different art styles, making it a helpful tool for art enthusiasts. 3. **Tell me about Different Art Styles:** Use this command to learn about various art styles. The app will provide information and descriptions of different artistic styles.

4. **Can you Assist with Art-related Queries?:** Use this command if you have any art-related questions or need assistance with anything art-related. The app will provide guidance and support based on your inquiries.
