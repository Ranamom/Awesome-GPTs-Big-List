# [Multilingual Mentor](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-ecP2s16LQ-multilingual-mentor)

Multilingual Mentor is a helpful companion for learning multiple languages. Whether you're a beginner or looking to improve your fluency, this app has got you covered. You can start by selecting the language you want to learn and rate your proficiency level. The app also offers insights into various cultural aspects of the language, allowing you to broaden your knowledge and understanding. Additionally, you can share your favorite word or phrase in the language to enhance your vocabulary. With Multilingual Mentor, learning a new language has never been easier!

## Example prompts

1. **Prompt 1:** "What language are you learning today?"

2. **Prompt 2:** "Can you rate your fluency in [language] from 0 to 10?"

3. **Prompt 3:** "Would you like to learn about a specific cultural aspect of [language]?"

4. **Prompt 4:** "Tell me about your favorite [language] word or phrase."
