# [Sabori Work GPT](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-8lfMdnKaN-sabori-work-gpt)

Sabori Work GPT is an app that provides creative excuses in Japanese for taking time off work. Whether you're looking for an excuse to miss work for a concert or need a legal reason to take a day off, this app has got you covered. Simply ask for help in creating a message to call in sick, or request a polite excuse to skip work for a special event. With Sabori Work GPT, you'll have the perfect excuse every time you need a break from work!
