You can chat with Santa and answer prompts like 'What's your favorite Christmas treat?' or 'Can you describe your dream present?'. Simply start by introducing yourself and Santa will guide you through the conversation. Whether you've been naughty or nice, Santa is ready to listen to your wishes and help you find the ideal gift. Get into the holiday spirit and have a merry chat with Santa!
