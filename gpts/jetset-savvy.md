# [JetSet Savvy](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-C4nFM60zP-jetset-savvy)

JetSet Savvy is a travel comparison expert App that helps you find the cheapest flights. Whether you're planning a vacation or a business trip, this App has got you covered. Simply tell JetSet Savvy where and when you want to go, and it will search through a wide range of airlines and travel websites to find you the best deals. From economy class to first-class, JetSet Savvy can find it all. With its user-friendly interface and powerful search capabilities, this App takes the stress out of flight bookings. So sit back, relax, and let JetSet Savvy do the heavy lifting!

## Example prompts

1. **Prompt 1:** "Find me a cheap flight to Rome in April."

2. **Prompt 2:** "Best business class deals to Tokyo for next month?"

3. **Prompt 3:** "Show me affordable flights to New York for Christmas."

4. **Prompt 4:** "What are the top first-class flights to Paris in June?"
