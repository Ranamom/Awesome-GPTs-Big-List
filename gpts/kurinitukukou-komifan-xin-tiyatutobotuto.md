# [クリニック口コミ返信チャットボット](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-T9T3x36Bs-kurinitukukou-komifan-xin-tiyatutobotuto)

A chatbot that helps clinics reply to reviews, from 1-star critiques to 5-star positive comments. Simply input the review and let the chatbot come up with the appropriate reply. Take the stress out of responding to customer feedback!

## Example prompts

1. **Prompt 1:** "こんにちは、どんな口コミが書き込まれましたか？私が返信を考えます。"

## Features and commands

1. `FindReply`: This command allows the ChatGPT app to find a suitable reply for a given user's review or comment. By providing details of the review or comment, the app can generate an appropriate response.
