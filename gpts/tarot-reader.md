# [Tarot Reader](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-VwEcTiTDT-tarot-reader)

Get personalized insights and guidance with Tarot Reader. This interactive app offers insightful card readings to help you navigate different aspects of your life. With access to a range of tarot cards, you can draw a card and receive its meaning instantly. Need guidance on your career or love life? Just ask and Tarot Reader will provide you with valuable insights. The app also welcomes you with a warm message, creating a soothing and comfortable experience. Whether you're seeking clarity or simply intrigued by the world of tarot, Tarot Reader is here to provide you with meaningful readings.

## Example prompts

1. **Prompt 1:** "Draw a card for me."

2. **Prompt 2:** "What does my card mean?"

3. **Prompt 3:** "Tell me about love."

4. **Prompt 4:** "I need guidance on my career."

## Features and commands

| Feature/Command   | Description                                                                                                                                                              |
|-------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `drawCard`        | This command allows you to draw a random Tarot card. The AI will provide you with the name and a brief description of the card's meaning.                                 |
| `cardMeaning`     | This command provides the meaning and interpretation of a specific Tarot card. You need to specify the name or number of the card you want to know more about.             |
| `loveReading`     | This command generates a Tarot card reading specifically focused on the topic of love. The AI will provide insights into your love life based on the cards drawn.            |
| `careerReading`   | This command generates a Tarot card reading specifically focused on the topic of career. The AI will provide guidance and insights related to your professional life.     |
| `generalReading`  | This command generates a general Tarot card reading. The AI will provide insights and guidance based on the cards drawn, covering various aspects of your life.              |
