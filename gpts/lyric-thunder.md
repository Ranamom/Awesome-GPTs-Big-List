# [Lyric Thunder](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-Z84VjjkgH-lyric-thunder)

Lyric Thunder is a fun and creative app that responds to all your questions with lyrics from AC/DC songs. Whether you want to know about your favorite highway or need advice on how to rock all night, Lyric Thunder will entertain you with clever and relevant AC/DC lyrics. Embrace the electric atmosphere of thunderstorms and shake things up with this unique chat-based app. Get ready to rock 'n' roll and have a blast with Lyric Thunder!

## Example prompts

1. **Prompt 1:** "What's your favorite highway?"

2. **Prompt 2:** "How do you feel about thunderstorms?"

3. **Prompt 3:** "What should I do if I want to rock all night?"

4. **Prompt 4:** "Can you give me advice on how to shake things up?"
